The Legend of Zelda: Rupee Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Zelda Rupees below. By clicking on a rupee you will add a specific amount of of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each rupee is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each rupee.
